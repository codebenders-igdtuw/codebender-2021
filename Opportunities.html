Opportunities

Internships, scholarships, mentorships, coding competitions, hackathons, open source programs, communities and a lot more.


WE Program

The program that identifies and prepares first year women students to become globally competitive software engineers


Generation Google Scholarship

Designed to help aspiring students pursuing computer science degrees excel in technology and become leaders in the field.


STEP Internship

STEP (Student Training in Engineering Program), formerly known as Engineering Practicum, is a 12-week internship for second-year undergraduate students with a passion for computer science.


Hash Code

A team programming competition, organized by Google, for students and professionals around the world


Code Jam

Google's longest running global coding competition, where programmers of all levels put their skills to the test.


Kick Start

A global year long online coding competition, consisting of three-hour rounds of a variety of algorithmic challenges designed by Google engineers


GSOC

A global program focused on bringing more student developers into open source software development.


MLSA

Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors


Codess

Codess is a community for female coders initiated by Microsoft. Codess was established to explore ways to promote gender diversity in the engineering field.


Girls Wanna Code

An initiative that started out in 2018 with an objective to promote women's tech talent and give them an opportunity to work with the best minds in technology in India.


GS Cup

Awarded with points on a scale of contribution you have made to the community. And the person with the most points will be the winner of the most exciting GS Cup.


GirlScript India Summit

GirlScript India Summit is an Annual celebration of technology, talent, entrepreneurship and diversity. It's a Two Days Event including Hackathon, Tech-Talks, Workshops.


LetsPy

LetsPy is a two day bootcamp for the people who are new to the language or want to brush up their skills.


GSSOC

GirlScript Summer of Code is the 3 month long Open Source program during summers conducted by GirlScript Foundation, started in 2018, with an aim to help beginners get started with Open Source Development while encouraging diversity.


The Uplift Project

A global project that aims at helping everyone looking for opportunities to leverage their knowledge, skills, and potential to higher levels.


Education Outreach Program

A scholarship program to make education reach everyone
