Who are Codebenders?

Codebenders is the oldest Programming Club of IGDTUW. We organize sessions on technical subjects and coding competitions to help students enhance their coding skills. We also give reminders on technical competitions. Most importantly, we believe in learning together.

Reach Everytime almost 100+ students attend and get benefits from our sessions

Sessions Successfully organized 4+ sessions on Placements preparations, project builiding and working towards more.

Active We are actively working towards helping students learn and grow for the last 1 year.

Codebenders' A team of 16 Codebenders works towards organizing these fruitful sessions

Skills

We, Codebenders believe apart from technical skills, social skills and personality development plays a huge role in the corporate sector. For achieving long-term goals one should have good communication skills and should be an active listener. So, we encourage students for their overall development.

Testimonials

Hear from our Presidents!

When I joined IGDTUW in my first year, I was looking for communities that aligned with my interests and values. Codebenders was a society that seemed to check all the boxes for me and I am glad I was able to join it. The past 4 years at Codebenders have been extremely fulfilling. I was able to be a part of a team that organised numerous sessions on different technologies, workshops and coding contests. Many of these events have ended with sweet messages from participants about how they found it helpful. Knowing that we are able to make a positive influence in the lives of students has been the biggest sources of motivation and happiness for our team. Looking forward to another year of exciting events, learning and sisterhood!

Ria arora

President & Codebender

Codebenders has been an important part of my four years journey in IGDTUW.It has helped me in my overall development. From attending the sessions organised by Codebenders in the first year to being part of the Codebenders team for three years, this journey has proven to be extremely fruitful. From learning as a junior to guiding my juniors and organising sessions, events and coding contests, I found a better version of myself. With an objective to explore, learn and grow together,we (Codebenders) are confident that our efforts of sharing our knowledge will help the students in their future and we could continue this further.

Komal kirti

President & Codebender

CodeBenders has been an essential part of my college life. The journey from a volunteer to President has been a rollercoaster ride filled with learnings and gratitude. When I reflect on my four years at CodeBenders, it brings back so many memories. I have met some amazing folks in these past four years at CodeBenders, who have guided me at each step and prepared me in unpredictable ways for life ahead. It’s been a long journey from attending workshops, sessions to organising them for the juniors. The sheer satisfaction that we get to see on their faces after conducting a successful event motivated us to keep moving forward towards our goal of inculcating a culture of coding in the university. I am extremely grateful to everyone who has been a part of this journey and All the best to the upcoming team!

Pallavi Bishnoi
